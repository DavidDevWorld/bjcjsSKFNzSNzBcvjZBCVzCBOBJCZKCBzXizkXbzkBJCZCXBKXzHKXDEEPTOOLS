Tips for Creating Great SEO Titles

Include Your Main Keyword

Place your primary keyword near the beginning of the title for better SEO ranking.

Keep Titles Under 60 Characters

Search engines typically display only the first 50-60 characters of a title in results.

Use Power Words

Words like "essential," "ultimate," "proven," and "secret" increase click-through rates.

Be Specific & Clear

Readers should immediately understand what your content offers from the title alone.
